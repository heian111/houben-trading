Who We Are

厚本贸易 (Houben Trading Co., Ltd.) is a professional sourcing company specializing in precision investment castings. We connect global buyers with high-quality Chinese foundry capabilities.

Based in Dongying, Shandong — close to Qingdao Port, one of the world's busiest ports — we manage the entire supply chain: supplier sourcing, quality inspection, logistics, and delivery.

Our team brings together deep knowledge of Chinese manufacturing with professional international trade expertise. We ensure every transaction is smooth, transparent, and reliable — so you can source from China with confidence.

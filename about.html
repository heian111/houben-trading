Who We Are

厚本贸易 (Houben Trading Co., Ltd.) is a professional sourcing company specializing in precision investment castings. We bridge the gap between China's world-class foundry capabilities and global industrial buyers.

Based in Dongying, Shandong — a major industrial hub close to Qingdao Port — we work directly with a leading foundry that has 30+ years of experience, a 200-person technical team, and over 20,000 product designs delivered worldwide.

Our foundry partner holds 44 patents, has co-authored 16 national industry standards for investment casting, and supplies customers across water treatment, oil & gas, marine, automotive, food processing, and heavy machinery sectors.
